How do I play?

Drag blocks from the bottom onto the grid. Complete full rows or columns to clear them and score points!

How do I get a high score?

Plan ahead and try to clear multiple lines at once for combo bonuses. The more lines you clear together, the higher your score!

Can I play offline?

Yes! Cube Blitz works completely offline. Play anywhere, anytime.

How do I continue after game over?

Watch a short video ad to clear some rows and keep playing. You can continue up to 3 times per game.

How do I remove ads?

Currently, ads help support the development of the game. A premium ad-free version may be available in the future.
