Privacy Policy

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Cube Blitz ("we", "our", or "the app") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application.

Last updated: December 2025

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information.

Cube Blitz does not require registration, login, or any personal data to play. Your game progress and high scores are stored locally on your device only.

Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services that may collect information:

Google AdMob

We display advertisements through Google AdMob. Google may collect and use data for personalized advertising. This may include:

  • Device identifiers
  • IP address
  • Usage data

For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

Data Storage

All game data (high scores, settings, progress) is stored locally on your device. We do not have access to this data, and it is not transmitted to any server.

Children's Privacy

Cube Blitz is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. The game does not require any personal information to play.

Your Rights

Since we don't collect personal data, there is no personal information to access, modify, or delete. You can reset your local game data by uninstalling the app.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
